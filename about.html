Designer

Hello, I'm Lewis.

In my approach to design, I am a firm believer that the most effective solutions often arise from well developed, minimalistic ideas. I embrace the opportunity to learn new skills and as such I am not afraid to make mistakes. The wide range of projects displayed within this portfolio exhibit my ability to fully immerse myself in the task at hand. An education in Architectural Design has provided me with the opportunity to use my transferable skills in other related fields, such as graphic, product and digital design. From the professional experience of working in a thriving architectural practice, I have developed further ability in time-management, teamwork and working under pressure. In my own time, I have further developed my skill set, self-teaching basic coding skills and learning relevant software as required. Many of my personal ventures have arisen from a problem or need I have identified. Whilst at university, I became increasingly aware of the inability to easily discover local happy hour deals. This led to the creation of Happyhour, a smartphone application which informs users of nearby drinks offers. I want to work with people that share the same passion, determination and vision as I do.
