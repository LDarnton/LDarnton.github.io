Bauhaus Posters


Bauhaus-Archiv Neueröffnung im Dezember 1979

This poster was designed to mark the opening of the Bauhaus-Archiv in Berlin. The building provides archival and exhibition space for the Bauhaus school, where related objects can be displayed and stored. The typographical form of the Joschmi font is utilised to portray the distinctive architecture.


Bauhaus-Archiv Wiedereröffnung 2022

This poster was designed to mark the opening of the extension of the Bauhaus-Archiv in Berlin. The extension is currently under construction and will open to the public in 2022. The poster portrays the extension as proposed by Staab Architeken in their design competition winning visualisations.
