Steven initially approached me with the intention of removing some outdated text and images from his website, which remained untouched since its creation in 2007. After looking at the site, I knew I could easily improve and update the aesthetic to a timeless modern design that reflected the character of his business.

Existing Website

The website was very dated...

Branding

The existing logo was quite dated and hadn't been updated since it was drawn up in the 1970s. I felt that it was quite important to modernise this and create a timeless, professional and modern look.

The colour scheme consists of a palette of greens that is complimented by a rich blue and varying accent shades of grey and white. The soft green evokes a positive feeling helping to harmonise the brand design. The less-saturated colours offer a soothing sensation and do not overwhelm the user, whilst still commanding a strong presence.

I worked up a few typographical logo options and presented these - after receiving feedback that number 5 was the preferred option, I began to develop it further.

The primary font was Playfair Display I experiemented with the font weight. The secondary font is an uppercase version of Roboto with an extended letter spacing.

Concept

Steven wanted the website to be informative, modern and low maintenance. It was important to consider who would be the audience of the website and what their intentions would be. The majority of the website users would be young adults looking to a rent a property in the York region - because of this it was important to optimise the design for mobile.

Due to the nature of the business, Steven didn't want to list specific/individual properties on the website, however wanted to give an example of select properties and a range of locations that accomdodation can be offered.

Website Design

The website consists of a green header bar, primary information is located here such as the company logo and quick access contact information.

The next segment of the website is dedicated to giving a lot of information without doing much, a tagline paired with an image (which has been given a parallax effect). The image depicts a couple being handed a set of keys, instantly informing the user of the type of business that can be expected on this domain, property rental. The accompanying text reads "We provide affordable rental properties" with subheading "York, England" which is self explanitory.

After this comes the experience section, this short paragraph informs the user about the business, how long it has been operating.
